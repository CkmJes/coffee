Daddy's Coffee

A Legacy of Perfect Brews Since 1989

Daddy's Coffee, established in 1989 by Mr. Gangadhar NM Nandkere in Chikmagalore, has grown from a local coffee roastery to a recognized name synonymous with quality and tradition...

The Founding and Early Years

In 1989, Mr. Gangadhar NM Nandkere embarked on a journey to create exceptional coffee in Chikmagalore, the heart of Indian coffee country. He began by meticulously roasting both Robusta and Arabica coffee beans, laying the foundation for what would become a cherished local brand. His dedication to quality and consistency quickly earned him a loyal following within the Chikmagalore community.

A New Generation Takes the Helm

In 2017, Dileep NG Gangadhar, Mr. Gangadhar's son, took over the reins of Daddy's Coffee. Continuing his father's legacy, Dileep brought fresh perspectives and innovative strategies while maintaining the core values of quality and tradition. His leadership has been instrumental in expanding the reach of Daddy's Coffee and solidifying its position in the competitive coffee market.

Rebranding and a Focus on Quality

In 2001, a significant milestone was achieved with the rebranding of the company to "Daddy's Coffee." This rebranding was more than just a name change; it was a declaration of the company's commitment to delivering a strong, flavorful, and unforgettable coffee experience. The phrase "brown gold" became synonymous with Daddy's Coffee, reflecting the rich, luxurious quality of their brews.

Serving a Diverse Clientele

Daddy's Coffee caters to a diverse range of customers across the Chikmagalore district and taluk. From local shops seeking a reliable source of high-quality coffee to tourists eager to experience the authentic flavors of Chikmagalore coffee, Daddy's Coffee has something to offer everyone. Their commitment to customer satisfaction and consistent quality has made them a preferred choice for both retail and wholesale clients.

Estate-Grown Excellence

One of the key differentiators of Daddy's Coffee is their ownership and operation of their own coffee estate. This allows them to maintain complete control over the quality of their beans, from cultivation to roasting. By sourcing only the best and freshest coffee beans directly from their estate in Chikmagalore, Daddy's Coffee ensures that every cup delivers an exceptional taste experience.

Our Story: A Legacy of Coffee Roasting

Our journey began in 1989 when Mr. Gangadhar NM Nandkere established Daddy's Coffee in Chikmagalur, a region renowned for its lush coffee plantations. With a deep understanding of coffee cultivation and a passion for the perfect roast, he embarked on a mission to deliver exceptional coffee to the local community. He started with a simple vision: to roast the best Robusta and Arabica beans, capturing the unique flavors of the region.

Over the years, Daddy's Coffee built a strong legacy in Chikmagalur, becoming a trusted name synonymous with quality and tradition. Mr. Gangadhar's dedication to his craft and commitment to sourcing the finest beans laid the foundation for the company's success.

Our Commitment to Quality

At Daddy's Coffee, quality is at the heart of everything we do. From sourcing the finest beans to perfecting the roasting process, we are dedicated to delivering an exceptional coffee experience.

  • Premium Beans: We carefully select the best Arabica and Robusta beans from our own coffee estate and trusted local growers in Chikmagalur. This ensures that only the highest quality beans are used in our roasting process.
  • Expert Roasting: Our roasting process is a blend of tradition and innovation. We use state-of-the-art equipment and time-honored techniques to bring out the unique flavors and aromas of each bean.
  • Freshness Guaranteed: We roast our beans in small batches to ensure maximum freshness. Our coffee is delivered to local shops, tourist destinations, and B2B partners throughout Chikmagalur district and taluk, ensuring that our customers always enjoy the freshest possible cup.

B2B Network and Bulk Ordering

Daddy's Coffee has developed a robust B2B network, making it a reliable supplier for businesses seeking high-quality coffee. They offer bulk ordering options, catering to the needs of cafes, restaurants, and other establishments. Their commitment to timely delivery and consistent quality has made them a trusted partner for businesses throughout the region.
